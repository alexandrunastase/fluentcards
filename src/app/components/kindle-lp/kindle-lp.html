1. Connect your Kindle to the computer

Only newer Kindles (2013+) are supported. Alternatively, browse our demo vocabulary.

2. Locate the vocab.db file on the Kindle disk.

On mac: /Volumes/Kindle/system/vocabulary/vocab.db

3. Drag & drop the vocab.db file here.

Your data will not be uploaded to any server. We'll process the file entirely locally in the browser.
